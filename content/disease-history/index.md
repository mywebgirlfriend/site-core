This section contains permanent documentation related to the history of my chronic illness to share with people that ask too often and i can't stand answer the same thing every single time. How it all began, signs and even confessions of stuff I had to do to get my diagnosis.


Typical content includes:

medical examinations;
imaging studies;
laboratory results;
medical reports;
clinical history;
treatment history;
supporting medical documentation.

Unlike the Blog, this section is documentary rather than chronological. Its content is organized manually and is intended to serve as a long-term reference rather than a sequence of dated posts.
